How_These_Metrics_Are_Computed

Activity Recognition Distribution is computed by running YOLOv8 Nano (trained on MS COCO — 330K images, 80 classes) on uniformly sampled frames from each video. Detected object classes (person=0, laptop=63, monitor=62, chair=56) are combined using scene heuristics to classify each frame as Presentation, Lecture, Meeting, or Discussion. The percentages shown are averaged across all your analyzed videos.
Neural Tone Consistency uses KeyBERT — a BERT-based keyword extraction model (all-MiniLM-L6-v2, trained on BooksCorpus + Wikipedia, 3.3B words) — to score keyword density in each 30-second transcript segment. Higher bars mean denser, more information-rich content. Combined with inter-frame motion energy (OpenCV optical flow proxy) for a multi-signal engagement score from 0–100.
